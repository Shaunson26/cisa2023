Rosa Fernández

Institute of Evolutionary Biology (CSIC-UPF, Barcelona)

Dr Rosa Fernández is a Ramón y Cajal researcher at the Institute of Evolutionary Biology (CSIC-UPF) in Barcelona, where she leads the Metazoa Phylogenomics Lab. Her research focuses on understanding how marine animals reshaped their genomes to give rise to animal terrestrial biodiversity. With a passion for unraveling the mysteries of how animals transitioned from aquatic to terrestrial environments, Dr. Fernández employs cutting-edge genomics and transcriptomics techniques to unlock the genetic secrets behind this remarkable evolutionary transition. Dr Fernández is also interested in reconstructing the Animal Tree of Life through phylogenomics approaches.
