Preliminary Program

Following previous symposia, the CISA 2023 symposium will be organized around four main topics:

  1. Taxonomy & Systematics
  2. DNA barcoding, Metabarcoding & eDNA
  3. Phylogeography and Population genetics
  4. Evolutionary Biology

The distribution of the different topics in the different sessions will not be done until we have the number of talks related to the different topics.
