Talks

  • The capacity of the assembly hall is limited to 90 people
  • The program and book of abstracts will be distributed in PDF but not printed on paper
  • A prize will be awarded for the best talk by a student (free course offered by Physalia and Transmitting Science)
  • We encourage all speakers to present their talks in English, although Sapnish or Portuguese may also be used

Posters

  • Given the limited space for the posters, only up to 30 poster presentations will be accepted.
  • A prize will be awarded for the best talk by a student (free course offered by Physalia and Transmitting Science)
  • We encourage all presenters to present their posters in English, although Spanish or Portuguese might also be used.
  • We may offer a service to print posters in Madrid.

Food

  • Registration includes coffee and drinks, the welcome reception with tapas and lunch
  • The congress dinner will be paid separately (€50)

Accommodation

  • The organization does not offer accommodation. The MNCN is located in the center of Madrid and there are numerous hotels around

Travel

  • Since Madrid is very well-connected by train with the rest of cities in the Iberian Peninsula (including Spain and Portugal), the use of train will be encouraged to all the attendees
  • Madrid has a well-developed network of public transport, including Metro, Bus and bicycles, that will allow the participants to move producing the lowest carbon footprint
